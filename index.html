Publications and Accepted Papers (Conferences)



IEEE-ICOECA,2024



B. Baranidharan, Janenie J. and Hardik Chhipa, "Light Weight CNN Based on Knowledge Distillation for Diabetic Retinopathy Detection," 2024 International Conference on Expert Clouds and Applications (ICOECA), Bengaluru, India, 2024, pp. 860-864, doi: 10.1109/ICOECA62351.2024.00151. keywords: Knowledge engineering;Diabetic retinopathy;Analytical models;Accuracy;Medical devices;Manuals;Blindness;Diabetic Retinopathy;Convolutional Neural Networks;Knowledge Distillation;Teacher Student model

This research addresses Diabetic Retinopathy (DR),a major cause of blindness in diabetics, by exploring various Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) for precise diagnosis. It introduces knowledge distillation to create a compact student model from complex teacher models, with DenseNet169 showing the highest accuracy. Using a Kaggle dataset, the study aims to enhance DR detection and contribute to combating this vision-threatening condition
